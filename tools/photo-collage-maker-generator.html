How to use the collage maker

  1. Click Add images or drag and drop your photos onto the canvas.
  2. Choose between Mosaic (organic, varied cell sizes) or Grid (uniform rows and columns).
  3. Pick a canvas size square for Instagram posts, 16:9 for wallpapers, 4:5 for Instagram portrait, and more.
  4. Use Shuffle layout to randomise the arrangement until you like it.
  5. Adjust the border size and colour to taste.
  6. Click Export PNG to download your finished collage.

Possible uses

  • Instagram posts: Combine multiple photos into a single 1:1 or 4:5 post.
  • Desktop wallpapers: Use the 16:9 preset and fill it with your favourite shots.
  • Mood boards: Arrange inspiration images into a neat layout for a project brief.
  • Event recaps: Collate event photos into a single shareable image.
  • Family albums: Create a mosaic of holiday or family photos to share or print.

Privacy

Your images never leave your device. This tool runs entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API no files are uploaded to any server, and nothing is stored anywhere. You can use it offline once the page has loaded.
